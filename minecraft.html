Minecraft With Noah

Noah's Getting Started Survival Tips

  1. Get as much wood as possible
  2. Get as much dirt as possible
  3. Get wood pickaxe
  4. Get stone and follow step four and make everything stone
  5. Make a crafting table, pickaxe, axe, spade and sword
  6. Make a wood door
  7. build a shack out of wood and dirt and put a door in the door frame and stay there for the night
  8. kill at least three white sheep
